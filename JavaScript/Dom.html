• Monitor the progress of your indexing requests in real-time. The status column provides insights into whether your pages are successfully indexed, pending, or if there are any issues that need to be addressed.
  • Take immediate action based on the status updates. For example, if a page indexing request fails, you can troubleshoot and resubmit the request.
  • Once you have selected the pages you wish to index, simply click on the "Index" button to initiate the process.
  • Grocliq handles all the technical details in the background. Your pages are sent to Google, and you don't need to worry about the complexities involved in the indexing process.
  • Typically, within minutes, you can see the results of your indexing request. Your pages will start appearing in Google search results, enhancing your visibility and reach.
